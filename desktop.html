Personal Finance Management Software Overview

A comprehensive desktop financial management system designed for expense tracking, budgeting, financial planning, cash flow control, recurring payments, and long-term financial analysis.

Area chart visualizing monthly savings and overspending behavior over time.

Recurring Expense Management

Interface for registering recurring expenses and splitting large annual payments into manageable monthly installments.

Future Obligations Projection

Line chart displaying pending obligations and scheduled payments across future months.

Expense Alert Configuration

Configuration panel for creating reminder alerts associated with specific expenses and due dates.

Expense Analytics Dashboard

Centralized interface used to generate charts and analyze personal spending behavior dynamically.

Monthly Expense Comparison

Line chart comparing expenses by month across selected years for total spending or individual categories.

Pie chart visualization of spending distribution for selected years and expense categories.

Heat map visualization showing expense intensity across all categories during the last 12 months.
