Community Requested Features Under Evaluation

The software already includes expense tracking, income management, budgeting tools, financial reports, charts, category analysis, cash flow monitoring, and many other personal finance features.

The following capabilities are currently being evaluated based on feedback and suggestions submitted by users.

Future Development Roadmap

1. Financial Independence (FIRE) Tracker

Track progress toward financial independence and estimate how many years remain until your passive income can cover living expenses.

2. Retirement Planner

Estimate future retirement readiness based on savings, investments, and expected expenses.

3. Emergency Fund Analyzer

Determine whether your emergency fund is sufficient to withstand income interruptions and unexpected expenses.

4. Future Net Worth Projection

Project future net worth using income growth, savings rates, investment returns, and inflation assumptions.

5. Expense Reduction Opportunities

Automatically identify spending categories that may offer the greatest potential savings.

6. Goal-Based Savings Planner

Track progress toward specific goals such as vacations, education, home purchases, or large future expenses.

7. What-If Financial Simulator

Simulate financial decisions before making them, such as changing income, reducing expenses, or increasing savings.

8. Subscription Expense Analyzer

Identify recurring subscriptions and estimate their long-term financial impact.

9. Lifestyle Inflation Tracker

Measure how spending habits evolve as income increases.

10. Financial Health Score

Generate a comprehensive score based on savings, debt levels, spending habits, and financial stability.

11. Debt Payoff Optimizer

Compare repayment strategies and estimate the fastest path toward becoming debt-free.

12. Cash Flow Forecast

Predict future cash balances based on expected income, recurring expenses, and savings plans.

13. Major Purchase Planner

Evaluate the financial impact of large purchases before committing to them.

14. Income Stability Analyzer

Measure dependency on specific income sources and assess financial resilience.

15. Inflation Impact Simulator

Estimate how inflation may affect future purchasing power and long-term financial goals.
